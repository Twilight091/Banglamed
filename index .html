Napa (Paracetamol)

Analgesic & Antipyretic - Beximco Pharmaceuticals

Overview

Napa is Bangladesh's most popular brand of Paracetamol manufactured by Beximco Pharmaceuticals. It is used for the treatment of fever, mild to moderate pain including headache, migraine, neuralgia, toothache, sore throat, and period pains.

Dosage Information

Standard Dose: 500-1000mg every 4-6 hours
Max Daily Dose: 4000mg for adults
Forms Available: Tablet, Suspension, Suppository, IV

Side Effects

Common side effects include:

  • Nausea
  • Stomach pain
  • Loss of appetite

Serious side effects: Liver damage (with overdose), allergic reactions. Seek medical help immediately if you experience yellowing of skin/eyes, dark urine, or difficulty breathing.

DGDA Information

Registration Number: DGDA-M-02165-0492-21

Manufacturing License: M-14, Beximco Industrial Park

Approval Date: 15 March 2021
